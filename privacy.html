Listen To Dream Privacy Policy

Effective June 25, 2026

Data collection

Listen To Dream does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. The app does not include advertising, analytics, tracking, accounts, or third-party data collection SDKs.

Files you import

Audio files and text-based PDF books that you import are copied into the app's local storage so they can be played or narrated. Imported files are not uploaded by Listen To Dream.

Reading and playback data

The app stores preferences, imported-file references, playback settings, and PDF reading progress locally on your device. This information is used only to provide app features such as resuming a book.

Speech

PDF narration uses Apple's on-device AVSpeechSynthesizer voices. Listen To Dream does not send PDF text to an external speech service.

Apple Watch

The Apple Watch app plays its bundled sounds locally and does not collect health, motion, location, or other personal data. Notification permission is requested only when you choose to set a Gentle Wake timer.

Deleting data

You can remove imported sounds and books from the Library. Deleting the app removes its locally stored data, subject to your device backup settings.

Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue in the public support repository.
